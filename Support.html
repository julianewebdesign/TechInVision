Technical Support

At TechInVision, we provide personalized and efficient technical support to keep your systems running smoothly and securely. Whether you're facing hardware issues, network problems, or need help with system configuration, we’re here to assist you remotely.

Our areas of support include:

Remote Troubleshooting

  • Live Diagnostics: Real-time issue identification and resolution.
  • System Analysis: In-depth checkups on your operating system and software.
  • Error Fixes: Resolve boot errors, crashes, and freezing issues.
  • User Support: Step-by-step help for common and advanced problems.

Device Setup

  • Initial Configuration: Setup of new desktops, laptops, or mobile devices.
  • Network Integration: Connect your device securely to Wi-Fi and printers.
  • App Installation: Installation and personalization of essential apps.
  • User Training: Basic usage training for non-technical users.

Maintenance & Optimization

  • System Cleaning: Removal of temporary files and performance hogs.
  • Performance Tuning: Optimize system startup and responsiveness.
  • Update Management: Ensure drivers and systems are up to date.
  • Backup Strategies: Set up local or cloud backups for data protection.

Connectivity Support

  • Wi-Fi Troubleshooting: Diagnose and fix connection drops or speed issues.
  • Peripheral Setup: Connect printers, scanners, monitors, and other devices.
  • Router Configuration: Secure and optimize your home or office network.
  • VPN & Remote Access: Set up secure connections for remote work.
