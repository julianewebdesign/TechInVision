Hardware

At TechInVision, we are experts in hardware solutions, working exclusively remotely to provide efficient and personalized technical support. Whether it's building custom PCs, upgrading your current setup, or diagnosing hardware issues, we're ready to assist you wherever you are.

See below how we can transform your technology experience with our remote services:

Custom PC Builds

  • Tailored Planning: We will analyze your needs and objectives in detail, whether you want to play the latest generation of games, perform intensive work such as video editing or simply have a reliable machine for your day-to-day work.
  • Component Selection: Complete assistance in selecting the most appropriate acceleration, motherboards, RAM, storage (HDDs and SSDs), power supplies, cases and cooling systems for the desired performance.
  • Custom Assembly: Creation of a unique PC, designed to meet your aesthetic preferences, from cases with RGB lighting to minimalist and silent designs.
  • Testing and Optimization: Ensuring that all the chosen components work in harmony, maximizing energy efficiency and performance.

Upgrades and Maintenance

  • Storage Expansion: Analysis and installation assistance for additional storage options, including high-capacity hard drives and ultra-fast NVMe SSDs, so you never run out of space.
  • Increased Performance: Recommendation and installation of upgrades such as faster RAM and modern, more powerful graphics cards to keep up with your growing demands.
  • Cooling and Silence: Solutions to replace noisy ventilation systems with quieter, more efficient options, such as high-performance coolers or liquid cooling.
  • Preventative Maintenance: Assistance with services that include cleaning internal components, reapplying thermal paste, and checking connections to ensure your PC continues to run like new.

Troubleshooting

  • Accurate Identification: Perform detailed analysis to identify problems such as overheating, boot failures, crashes, or slow performance.
  • Step-by-Step Guidance: Provide a clear plan for fixing problems, whether it's replacing faulty parts or adjusting system settings.
  • Performance Analysis: Tools and techniques for testing hardware, verifying the integrity and functionality of individual components.

Remote Support

  • Installation Guides: Specific assistance for installing new components, such as SSDs, video cards or memory kits, with clear explanations for each step.
  • Settings and Updates: Helps you configure the BIOS, install updated drivers and adjust the operating system to get the most out of your hardware.
  • Immediate Assistance: Remote technical support to answer questions in real time, solve problems quickly and ensure you have full control over your PC.
