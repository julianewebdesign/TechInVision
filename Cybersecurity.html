Cybersecurity

With the rise of digital threats, cybersecurity is essential to protect your data, systems, and privacy. At TechInVision, we offer complete cybersecurity solutions tailored to your environment, combining prevention, detection, and education.

Our cybersecurity services include:

Threat Assessment

  • Comprehensive Evaluation: Identify vulnerabilities in systems, software, and networks.
  • Threat Detection: Detect malware, ransomware, and possible breaches early.
  • Detailed Reports: Receive clear documentation with practical remediation strategies.
  • Strategic Planning: Implement secure policies, updates, and configurations.

Data Protection

  • Encryption: Safeguard sensitive data from unauthorized access.
  • Secure Backups: Configure encrypted backups to prevent data loss.
  • Access Control: Define permissions and minimize exposure.
  • Data Recovery: Assist in recovery after cyber incidents.

Network Security

  • Firewall Configuration: Block unwanted access with custom rules.
  • Intrusion Monitoring: Real-time alerts for unusual activities.
  • Wi-Fi Hardening: Secure your wireless environment from intruders.
  • VPN Setup: Encrypt your connection and maintain anonymity.

Security Training

  • Best Practices: Teach strong password creation, 2FA, and safe habits.
  • Simulated Attacks: Test system resilience and employee awareness.
  • Security Culture: Build long-term cybersecurity mindset and readiness.
